About

I am a doctoral candidate in the School of Public Affairs and Administration at Rutgers University–Newark. My research interests encompass public management, social equity, institutional theory, human resource management, and computational social science. My works have been published at Public Administration Review, Administrative Theory & Praxis, and others.
